Hokkaido

📍 Hokkaido, Japan

🌤️ Best Months: December – February

Hokkaido is Japan's northernmost island, known for its natural beauty and outdoor activities. In winter, it offers excellent skiing and snowboarding opportunities, while also providing a chance to see the Northern Lights. The island is home to national parks, hot springs, and unique wildlife.

🎯 Things to do:

  • Skiing
  • Snowboarding
  • Visit hot springs
  • See the Northern Lights
  • Explore national parks
  • Experience local culture
