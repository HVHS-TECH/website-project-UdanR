Atacama Desert

📍 Location: Chile

🌤️ Best Months: December – March

The Atacama Desert is one of the driest places on Earth, known for its otherworldly landscapes and stargazing opportunities. In winter, the desert offers clear skies and cooler temperatures, making it an ideal destination for astronomy enthusiasts and nature lovers. Visitors can explore salt flats, geysers, and unique ecosystems.
