Eiffel Tower

Location📍: Paris, France

Best Seasons: Spring (Apr-Jun) & Autumn (Sep-Oct)

An iconic iron tower rising above Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.

Built in 1889 for the World’s Fair, the Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable landmarks on Earth. Visitors can take elevators to observation decks for panoramic views of Paris. At night, the tower sparkles with thousands of lights every hour.

Great Wall

Location📍: Northern China

Best Seasons: Spring & Autumn

An ancient wall stretching thousands of kilometers across mountains and valleys.

The Great Wall was built over centuries to protect Chinese kingdoms from invasions. It stretches more than 21,000 km. Some of the most popular sections for visitors include Badaling and Mutianyu near Beijing.
